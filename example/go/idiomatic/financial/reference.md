Business
    - [Corporate Charter](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/c/corporatecharter.asp)
        - a.k.a [Articles of Incorporation](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/a/articlesofincorporation.asp)
    -   the corporate charter includes:
        -   the corporation's name
        -   its purpose
        -   whether the corporation is a for-profit or nonprofit institution
        -   the location of the corporation
        -   the number of shares that are authorized to be issued
        -   the names of the parties involved in the formation
